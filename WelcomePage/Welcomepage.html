Built with Trust

Alva's Health Centre (AHC) stands as a beacon of excellence in Moodbidri,renowned for its unwavering commitment to providing world-class medical care tailored to the needs of underprivileged individuals. Since its inception in 1986 ,AHC has grown from a modest facility to a distinguished 200-bedded hospital offering comprehensive healthcare services.
